Frequently Asked Questions

What training program is best for building muscle mass as a beginner with limited equipment?

Our Strength Training Program can be adapted for beginners with limited equipment. We focus on bodyweight exercises and progressive overload principles that work with minimal equipment. Contact us to discuss your specific situation and equipment availability.

How can I contact FitXcel for personalized training consultation?

You can reach us through the contact form above, by email at info@fitxcel.com, or by phone at (555) 123-4567. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your goals and recommend the best program for you.

What are the key principles of effective strength training?

The key principles include progressive overload, proper form and technique, adequate recovery, consistent training, and proper nutrition. Our Strength Training Program covers all these principles in detail with structured workout plans.
