About Kyoto

Kyoto served as Japan's capital for over 1,000 years and is renowned for its classical Buddhist temples, stunning gardens, imperial palaces, and traditional wooden houses. The city is also famous for its formal geisha districts and traditional arts.

With 17 UNESCO World Heritage Sites, Kyoto offers an unparalleled glimpse into Japan's rich cultural heritage while seamlessly blending ancient traditions with modern urban life.
